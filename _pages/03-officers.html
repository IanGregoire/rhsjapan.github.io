Asuka Hayashi

Secretary

My parents didn't know what to name me and went to a priest for guidance. "Asuka Hayashi", the priest said. In Japanese this would be 飛鳥 and the characters mean flying bird.

My name is also an era in Japanese history and when I'd go to a new school they would remember my name by calling me Hayashi rice, similar to curry rice, from the Asuka era. I joined Japan Club because I love the culture there.
